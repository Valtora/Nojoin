## Context

What forced this decision? Describe the problem, the constraints, and which core contract is affected (trust boundary, persistence, capture, processing, or deployment). State the facts and the pressures, not the solution.

## Decision

The decision, stated in active voice: "We will ...". Be specific enough that a reader can tell whether a later change is consistent with it.

## Consequences

What becomes easier, harder, or constrained as a result. Include the trade-offs accepted, any new obligations on contributors or operators, and follow-up work that the decision implies.

## Alternatives Considered

The options that were weighed and why they were not chosen. One short paragraph or list per alternative is enough.
